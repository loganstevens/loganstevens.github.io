Hi! I am an incoming Ph.D. student in the Embodied Dynamic Laboratory (EmD) advised by Dr. Jun Nishida. My research area is at the intersection of HCI, XR, learning science, and cognitive neuroscience. Historically, my research has been in XR and education; seeing how immersion in XR environments affects the learning process. Recently, I have expanded this by factoring in HCI more broadly, as well as other cognitive effects during the learning process.
